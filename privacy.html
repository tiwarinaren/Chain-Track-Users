Privacy Policy for Chain-Track

Last updated: April 7, 2025

This privacy policy explains how Chain-Track ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our habit tracking app.

Information We Collect

  • Your email address when you sign in with Google or email/password.
  • Habit data you create, including habit names, categories, streaks, and notes.
  • Profile information you provide, such as usernames and avatars.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and synchronize your habit tracking data across devices.
  • To personalize your experience within the app.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase Realtime Database, protected by Firebase's security measures.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for authentication and data storage. Your use of the app is also subject to Google's privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Chain-Track is intended for users over the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
